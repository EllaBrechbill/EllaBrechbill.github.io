Kenton Wenger Lives in Lititz, PA and is a Junior at Warwick High School. The idea for TOO MANY TACOS came from him and a friend's mind in the summer of 2020! He is the owner and creator of TOO MANY TACOS.

Too Many Tacos

TOO MANY TACOS is a brand brought to you by Kenton Wenger. It started as an inside joke between him and some friends in the summer of 2020. It is now the home of all your Jesus merchandise needs! The goal of TOO MANY TACOS is to allow for an easy way to start conversations about your faith using something that everybody loves: interesting and funky clothes made just for you!
